MY HOBBY

Speaking of boldness, I’ve always been someone who loves a good adventure. These activities remind me that life itself is an adventure — full of challenges, risks, and breathtaking moments at the top. One of my favorite places to hike is the Rawang Bypass trail, which offers stunning views of the forest and city skyline. I’ve already hiked there about three times, and each trip felt like a new experience, pushing my limits both physically and mentally. These adventures give me a sense of freedom, help me stay active, and teach me the importance of perseverance and appreciating the beauty of nature along the journey.
